My becode story

I started looking on the www and found multiple options, all in coding. I was too late to go to the Becode in Antwerp so I chose to come here instead of waiting half a year. The good thing about comming to Brussels is that I do not only work on my coding skills, but learn French as well. Something with two birds and a stone or stuff. Now I hope that I can do very well on the excercises and will become a master coder. Ganbaru!!
